About the Busy Bee Team

We developed this site to address a gap in the to-do list market. Our site combines the essential properties of existing to-do list apps with the connectivity of social media platforms and the power of business work management systems. Our application is designed with the user experience in mind. With minimal setup time (less than 5 minutes) and responsive design, users can bring their hive with them wherever they go and focus on getting things done.

Check out our documention on GitHub

Out team came together in a coding bootcamp sponsored by UNC Chapel Hill. This site was developed to appease the coding gods and to fulfill coursework requirements.
